## ACT 1 — ESCALATION (Pages 1–40)

### Scene 1 — BANK HEIST (pp. 1–11)
**Location:** Gotham National Bank (exterior rooftop → interior vault → lobby → street)
**Characters:** Joker (as "Bozo"), Grumpy, Chuckles, Happy, Dopey, Bus Driver, Bank Manager (William Fichtner)
**Beat:** Six clowns rob a mob bank. Each kills the one before per Joker's plan. Joker reveals himself last — mask off, grenade in Bank Manager's mouth, bus escape into school convoy.
**Key Lines:**
- GRUMPY: "I know why they call him the Joker."
- BANK MANAGER: "Think you're smart, huh? The guy that hired you, he'll just do the same to you."
- JOKER: "Whatever doesn't kill you simply makes you... stranger."
**Visual Intel:** 6 named clown masks. Happy drills vault BAREFOOT (sneakers on hands). Vault wired with 5,000 volts. Joker pulls grenade + purple thread from jacket lining. Bus crashes through wall, joins school convoy.

---

### Scene 2 — SCARECROW BUST (pp. 12–17)
**Location:** Multi-story parking garage → rooftop
**Characters:** Batman, Scarecrow (Crane), Chechen, fake Batmen (copycats), Rachel Dawes
**Beat:** Mob deal interrupted. Batman takes down Scarecrow + Chechen but gets mauled by Rottweilers. Copycats in hockey pads try to help. Batman's suit is damaged.
**Key Lines:**
- COPYCAT: "What gives you the right? What's the difference between you and me?"
- BATMAN: "I'm not wearing hockey pads."
**Visual Intel:** Batman's suit has bite damage. Rottweilers = Chechen's dogs. Copycats wear homemade costumes.

---

### Scene 3 — WAYNE/FOX — NEW SUIT (pp. 17–19)
**Location:** Wayne Enterprises Applied Sciences → Batcave
**Characters:** Bruce Wayne, Lucius Fox, Alfred
**Beat:** Wayne requests lighter armor (trade protection for speed). Fox presents new suit. Alfred stitches Wayne's dog bite wounds.
**Key Lines:**
- FOX: "It'll stop a knife... but it'll make you more vulnerable to gunfire."
- WAYNE: "I'll be faster."
- ALFRED: "Know your limits, Master Wayne."
- WAYNE: "Batman has no limits."
- ALFRED: "Well, you do, sir."
**Visual Intel:** New suit = more segmented, greater mobility. Alfred's medical kit in the penthouse.

---

### Scene 4 — MOB MEETING + LAU (pp. 19–23)
**Location:** Restaurant kitchen (mob meeting)
**Characters:** Maroni, Chechen, Gambol, Lau (via TV screen), Joker
**Beat:** Mob bosses meet. Lau (via satellite from Hong Kong) says he's moved their money offshore for safety. Joker enters uninvited, offers to kill Batman for half their money. Gambol puts a bounty on him. Joker kills Gambol's men with his "magic trick" (pencil through the skull).
**Key Lines:**
- JOKER: "How about a magic trick? I'm going to make this pencil... disappear."
- JOKER: "If we don't deal with this now, soon little Gambol here won't be able to get a nickel for his grandma."
- JOKER: "I'm a man of my word."
- JOKER: "Let's not blow this out of proportion..."
- JOKER: "If you're good at something, never do it for free."
**Visual Intel:** Joker enters in purple coat, greasy green hair, Glasgow smile scars. Pencil trick = pencil driven through thug's eye into table.

---

### Scene 5 — HARVEY DENT INTRODUCTION (pp. 23–27)
**Location:** Gotham courtroom → MCU hallway → Gordon's office → rooftop (bat-signal)
**Characters:** Harvey Dent, Rachel Dawes, Gordon, Batman
**Beat:** Dent prosecutes a mob witness who pulls a gun — it misfires (Chinese-made). Dent punches him. Rachel and Dent are dating. Gordon and Dent clash over trust/corruption in GCPD. Batman meets Dent via Gordon on the rooftop.
**Key Lines:**
- DENT: "I don't think you'll need it... carbon fiber. 28 caliber. Made in China. If you want to kill a public servant, I'd recommend something American-made."
- DENT (to Gordon): "If I didn't work with cops you investigated, I'd be... working alone."
- BATMAN: "The Joker's just a taste of things to come. We have to fix what's wrong."
**Visual Intel:** Double-headed coin first shown. Dent is handsome, confident, idealistic. Bat-signal scene = three men planning strategy.

---

### Scene 6 — JOKER KILLS GAMBOL (pp. 27–29)
**Location:** Gambol's office/pool hall
**Characters:** Joker, Gambol, bounty hunters
**Beat:** Bounty hunters deliver "dead" Joker in a body bag. Joker springs alive, kills Gambol with knife. First scar story: "My father was a drinker and a fiend..."
**Key Lines:**
- JOKER: "Wanna know how I got these scars? My father was a drinker... and a fiend."
- JOKER: "Now I see the funny side. Now I'm always smiling."
**Visual Intel:** Joker played dead in body bag. Knife held inside cheek of victim. Scar story #1 = abusive father version.

---

### Scene 7 — LAU EXTRACTION FROM HONG KONG (pp. 30–38)
**Location:** Wayne penthouse → Hong Kong (LSI Holdings tower) → Gotham
**Characters:** Bruce Wayne, Alfred, Fox, Lau, Hong Kong security
**Beat:** Wayne/Fox travel to Hong Kong for "business." Fox plants sonar device in LSI Holdings. Batman skyhooks Lau off the building using a C-130 cargo plane. Lau brought back to Gotham.
**Key Lines:**
- FOX: "Let me get this straight. You think your client, one of the wealthiest men in the world, is spending his nights beating criminals to a pulp with his bare hands... and your plan is to blackmail this person?"
- ALFRED: "I trust you don't have me followed on my day off?"
**Visual Intel:** Skyhook extraction = Batman grabs Lau, balloon lifts both to C-130. Hong Kong skyline at night. Fox's sonar phone planted as surveillance device.

---

### Scene 8 — RICO + MASS ARRESTS (pp. 38–42)
**Location:** MCU interrogation room → courthouse → Gotham streets
**Characters:** Dent, Rachel, Gordon, Lau, Mayor Garcia
**Beat:** Lau flips — offers all mob clients for immunity. Dent files RICO charges. 549 criminals arrested simultaneously. Judge Surrillo finds Joker card in case files. Mayor warns Dent about 18-month exposure.
**Key Lines:**
- DENT: "If their money was pooled, we can charge every one of them."
- MAYOR: "Dent, I'm sorry but... 18 months of clean streets?"
**Visual Intel:** Mass arrest montage. Joker card tucked into Surrillo's case files — playing card with crude additions.

### Scene 9 — FAKE BATMAN + JOKER'S ULTIMATUM (pp. 42–45)
**Location:** City Hall exterior → Brian Douglas video (TV broadcast)
**Characters:** Joker (via video), Brian Douglas (fake Batman), Mayor, Gordon
**Beat:** Fake Batman (Brian Douglas) hung dead from flagpole with Joker card: "WILL THE REAL BATMAN PLEASE STAND UP?" Joker's videotaped message: Batman must unmask or people die, one a day.
**Key Lines:**
- JOKER (video): "Tell them your name..." / BRIAN: "Brian Douglas..." / JOKER: "Are you the real Batman?"
- JOKER: "Batman must take off his mask and turn himself in. Every day he doesn't, people will die. Starting tonight. I'm a man of my word."
**Visual Intel:** Body hung from flagpole in Batman costume. Joker card pinned to chest. Video is shaky, handheld, terrifying.

## ACT 2A — THE TEST BEGINS (Pages 45–80)

### Scene 10 — WAYNE FUNDRAISER FOR DENT (pp. 45–50)
**Location:** Wayne penthouse
**Characters:** Bruce Wayne, Harvey Dent, Rachel Dawes, Alfred, guests
**Beat:** Wayne hosts fundraiser for Dent. Alfred and Dent bond ("any psychotic ex-boyfriends?"). Wayne arrives with supermodels, endorses Dent publicly. Wayne and Rachel on balcony — she says the day Batman ends is coming. Simultaneously: Gordon learns Joker DNA matches Surrillo, Dent, and Loeb — all three targeted.
**Key Lines:**
- ALFRED: "A little liquid courage, Mr. Dent?"
- DENT: "Rachel talks about you all the time."
- WAYNE: "I believe in Harvey Dent."
- RACHEL: "The day you told me about... Batman. It's coming."
**Visual Intel:** Penthouse party. Wayne arrives with women on each arm. Dent is charming, genuine.

---

### Scene 11 — SIMULTANEOUS ATTACKS: SURRILLO + LOEB (pp. 50–52)
**Location:** Surrillo's car → Commissioner Loeb's office
**Characters:** Judge Surrillo, Commissioner Loeb, Gordon
**Beat:** Surrillo's car explodes (Joker card with "UP" left inside). Loeb poisoned via acid in whiskey bottle. Two institutions hit simultaneously.
**Visual Intel:** Car explosion. Loeb collapses mid-toast, face dissolving. Gordon arrives too late to both.

### Scene 12 — DENT/RACHEL — THE PROPOSAL (pp. 52–53)
**Location:** Dent's apartment kitchen
**Characters:** Harvey Dent, Rachel Dawes
**Beat:** Dent proposes to Rachel. She doesn't answer — uncertain.
**Key Lines:**
- DENT: "What's your answer?"
- RACHEL: "I don't have an answer."
**Visual Intel:** Intimate domestic scene. Double-headed coin shown again during proposal.

### Scene 13 — JOKER CRASHES THE FUNDRAISER (pp. 53–60)
**Location:** Wayne penthouse
**Characters:** Joker, Rachel, Batman, Wayne, party guests, thugs
**Beat:** Joker and thugs crash the party looking for Dent. Wayne hides Dent (sleeper hold). Joker tells scar story #2 (wife version) to Rachel. Rachel slugs him. Batman arrives, fights Joker and thugs. Joker's shoe blade penetrates armor. Joker drops Rachel out window. Batman dives, saves her on taxi roof. Joker escapes — "I'm a man of my word" about not killing Dent.
**Key Lines:**
- JOKER: "Good evening, ladies and gentlemen. We are tonight's entertainment."
- JOKER (scar story #2): "I had a wife... She tells me I worry too much... 'Why so serious?'"
- JOKER: "A little fight in you. I like that." / BATMAN: "Then you're going to love me."
**Visual Intel:** Joker in purple coat among tuxedos. Shoe blade between armor plates. Rachel falling = Batman cape-dives after her. Land on taxi.

### Scene 14 — ALFRED'S BURMA STORY (pp. 60–62)
**Location:** Wayne penthouse
**Characters:** Alfred, Bruce Wayne
**Beat:** Wayne asks how to fight someone who just wants chaos. Alfred tells the Burma bandit story — a man who stole rubies and threw them away because it wasn't about money.
**Key Lines:**
- ALFRED: "Some men aren't looking for anything logical, like money. They can't be bought, bullied, reasoned, or negotiated with. Some men just want to watch the world burn."
- WAYNE: "How did you catch him?" / ALFRED: "We burned the forest down."
**Visual Intel:** Quiet penthouse scene. Two men, low light. This is the thematic key.

### Scene 15 — CRIME SCENE + BULLET FORENSICS (pp. 62–66)
**Location:** Apartment crime scene → Bat-bunker
**Characters:** Batman, Gordon, Fox
**Beat:** Dead men with Joker cards. Driver's licenses: "Patrick Harvey" and "Richard Dent" = anagram for HARVEY DENT (next target). Batman extracts bullet from brick wall for fingerprint reconstruction. Reese discovers Batman's identity via Tumbler blueprints — Fox shuts him down.
**Key Lines:**
- FOX (to Reese): "Let me get this straight. You think your client, one of the wealthiest men in the world, is spending his nights beating criminals to a pulp with his bare hands... and your plan is to blackmail this person? Good luck."
**Visual Intel:** Forensic detail — bullet extracted from brick. Bat-bunker has fingerprint reconstruction tech. MV Augusta Brutale motorcycle.

### Scene 17 — DENT'S COIN FLIP INTERROGATION (pp. 72–74)
**Location:** Back alley / car
**Characters:** Dent, Joker's thug (Schiff), Batman
**Beat:** Dent snaps — uses his coin flip to interrogate Joker's paranoid schizophrenic thug. Double-headed coin = no real chance. Batman intervenes. Dent's darkness emerging.

### Scene 18 — "I AM THE BATMAN" (pp. 74–78)
**Location:** Wayne penthouse → bat-bunker → press conference (Gotham City Hall)
**Characters:** Wayne, Alfred, Rachel, Dent, press
**Beat:** Wayne decides to turn himself in. Alfred: "Endure, Master Wayne... he can be the outcast." Alfred burns documents, closes bat-bunker. Rachel gives Alfred the letter (unsealed). At press conference, Dent says "I am the Batman" to protect Wayne.
**Key Lines:**
- ALFRED: "Endure, Master Wayne. Take it. They'll hate you for it, but that's the point of Batman. He can be the outcast. He can make the choice that no one else can make. The right choice."
- DENT: "I am the Batman."
**Visual Intel:** Press conference chaos. Rachel visits Dent in cell — double-headed coin reveal. Letter given to Alfred (crucial later).

### Scene 19 — DENT CONVOY CHASE (pp. 78–84)
**Location:** Gotham streets — lower fifth → tunnel → highway
**Characters:** Dent (prisoner transport), Joker, Batman, Gordon (revealed alive)
**Beat:** Joker attacks convoy with garbage truck, RPG, machine guns. Batmobile destroyed by RPG — Bat-Pod emerges from wreckage. Epic chase through mall. Bat-Pod cables flip Joker's truck. Joker plays chicken: "Hit me." Batman dumps bike. Gordon reveals himself alive, captures Joker. Gordon promoted to Commissioner.
**Key Lines:**

## ACT 2B — THE FALL (Pages 84–120)

### Scene 20 — THE INTERROGATION (pp. 84–92)
**Location:** MCU holding/interrogation room
**Characters:** Batman, Joker, Gordon
**Beat:** THE central scene. Joker reveals: no matches on prints, DNA, dental. Custom clothing, no labels. Batman enters, lights come on. Philosophical showdown. Joker reveals Harvey and Rachel are at separate locations — gives addresses SWITCHED. Batman races for Rachel (but finds Dent). Gordon races for Dent (but finds Rachel). The cruelest trick.
**Key Lines:**
- JOKER: "You have nothing. Nothing to threaten me with. Nothing to do with all your strength."
- JOKER: "Don't talk like one of them. You're not. Even if you'd like to be."
- JOKER: "I don't want to kill you. What would I do without you?"
- JOKER: "You. Complete. Me."
- JOKER: "Their morals, their code... it's a bad joke. Dropped at the first sign of trouble. They're only as good as the world allows them to be."
- JOKER: "I'm not a monster. I'm just ahead of the curve."
- JOKER: "Tonight you're going to break your one rule..."
- BATMAN: "Which address is which?" / JOKER: "250 52nd Boulevard. And she's on Avenue X at Cicero."
**Visual Intel:** Dark room, single light. Batman slams Joker's face on table. Joker laughing through pain. Door barricaded with chair.

### Scene 21 — RACHEL DIES / TWO-FACE BORN (pp. 92–96)
**Location:** Two warehouses — 250 52nd (Rachel's) + Avenue X (Dent's)
**Characters:** Batman, Rachel, Dent, Gordon, Joker (escaping MCU)
**Beat:** Batman finds DENT at 250 52nd (not Rachel — addresses were switched). Rachel dies in explosion at the other location. Fireball ignites diesel on Dent's left side. Half his face burned. Two-Face is born. Simultaneously: Joker escapes MCU using phone bomb hidden in fat thug's belly.
**Key Lines:**
- RACHEL: "Okay. (very calm) Okay."
- DENT (as building burns): Screaming, tied to chair, diesel barrels igniting on his left side
**Visual Intel:** Rachel alone in dark warehouse, barrels of fuel. Dent strapped to chair, fuel on left side. Massive explosion. Dent's left face = fully exposed musculature.

### Scene 22 — JOKER BURNS THE MONEY (pp. 96–99)
**Location:** Warehouse (mob money storage)
**Characters:** Joker, Chechen, Lau (tied to money pile)
**Beat:** Joker burns the entire mob fortune — billions of dollars — with Lau tied on top. Kills Chechen, feeds him to his own dogs. This is the Joker's thesis statement: it was never about money.
**Key Lines:**
- JOKER: "It's not about money. It's about sending a message. Everything. Burns."
- JOKER (to Chechen): "I'm a man of my word."
**Visual Intel:** Mountain of cash burning. Lau screaming on top. Dogs. Joker silhouetted against fire.

### Scene 23 — RACHEL'S LETTER + ALFRED'S CHOICE (pp. 99–100)
**Location:** Wayne penthouse → Alfred's quarters
**Characters:** Alfred, Rachel (V.O.)
**Beat:** Alfred reads Rachel's letter. She chose Harvey — not Bruce. She was never going to wait. Alfred decides to hide the letter from Wayne to protect him.
**Key Lines:**

### Scene 24 — HOSPITAL: TWO-FACE + JOKER (pp. 100–106)
**Location:** Gotham General Hospital — Dent's room → exterior
**Characters:** Dent/Two-Face, Joker (disguised as nurse)
**Beat:** Dent sees his scarred coin, rips off bandages. Reese threatens to reveal Batman on TV. Joker calls in: kill Reese in 60 minutes or he blows up a hospital. Gordon evacuates. Joker as nurse visits Dent. THE HOSPITAL SPEECH — Joker converts Dent to chaos. Dent flips coin for Joker — lives. Joker blows up Gotham General.
**Key Lines:**
- JOKER: "I'm a dog chasing cars. I wouldn't know what to do with one if I caught it."
- JOKER: "Do I really look like a guy with a plan?"
- JOKER: "I just did what I do best. I took your little plan and I turned it on itself."
- JOKER: "Introduce a little anarchy. Upset the established order. And everything becomes chaos. I'm an agent of chaos."
- JOKER: "It's fair."
**Visual Intel:** Joker in nurse disguise (wig, scrubs). Dent's face = half-burned, exposed tendons. Coin = scarred on one side. Hospital explosion behind walking Joker (he fumbles with detonator — pauses — then it goes).

## ACT 3 — THE DARK KNIGHT (Pages 121–141)

### Scene 27 — PREWITT BUILDING ASSAULT (pp. 121–131)
**Location:** Prewitt Building (penthouse + stairwells + exterior) → rooftop opposite
**Characters:** Batman, Joker, Gordon, SWAT teams, hostages/clowns, Rottweilers, Engel
**Beat:** Batman locates Joker via sonar. THE TWIST: Joker dressed hostages as clowns (weapons duct-taped to hands, masks, mouth taped) and dressed his men as "doctors" and "patients." SWAT targets the hostages. Batman fights to protect them — yanks clowns from windows, fights SWAT, disarms sniper with grapple gun. Then enters to face Joker. Dogs attack. Joker stabs Batman in ribs. Fight on steel beam. Ferry experiment: vote is 340-196 FOR detonating prisoner ferry. Businessman picks up remote but can't press it. Tattooed Prisoner takes remote from Warden — THROWS IT OUT THE WINDOW. Neither ferry detonates. Midnight passes. Joker loses.
**Key Lines:**
- BATMAN: "It's not that simple. With the Joker, it never is."
- BATMAN (sonar): "Fox. I need picture."
- JOKER: "You came. I'm touched."
- JOKER: "All the old familiar places."
- JOKER: "If we don't stop fighting, we're going to miss the fireworks."
- BATMAN: "There won't be any fireworks."
- BATMAN: "What were you hoping to prove? That deep down, we're all as ugly as you?"
- BATMAN: "You're alone."
- JOKER: "Can't rely on anyone these days. Have to do everything yourself."
- JOKER: "You know how I got these scars?" / BATMAN: "No. But I know how you get these—" (scallop blades fire)
**Visual Intel:** Sonar vision = blue pulsing x-ray. Hostages in clown masks with weapons taped to hands. SWAT rappelling. Batman fights both sides. Final fight on steel beam, high above city. Joker hanging upside down, laughing.

### Scene 28 — THE ACE IN THE HOLE (pp. 131–132)
**Location:** Prewitt Building (Joker hanging upside down)
**Characters:** Batman, Joker
**Beat:** Joker reveals his real victory: Harvey Dent. "I took Gotham's white knight. And I brought him down to my level."
**Key Lines:**
- JOKER: "You truly are incorruptible, aren't you? You won't kill me out of some misplaced sense of self-righteousness... and I won't kill you because you're too much fun. We're going to do this forever."
- BATMAN: "You'll be in a padded cell, forever."
- JOKER: "This city just showed you it's full of people ready to believe in good." / BATMAN: "This city just showed you it's full of people ready to believe in good."
- JOKER: "Until they find out what I did with the best of them. Until they get a good look at the real Harvey Dent."
- JOKER: "I took Gotham's white knight. And I brought him down to my level. It wasn't hard — madness is like gravity. All it takes is a little push."
**Visual Intel:** Joker upside down, laughing. Batman's face — realization. SWAT approaching below.

---

### Scene 29 — TWO-FACE'S LAST STAND (pp. 132–137)
**Location:** Burnt warehouse, 52nd Street (where Rachel died)
**Characters:** Dent/Two-Face, Gordon, Gordon's family (Barbara, James Jr., little girl), Batman
**Beat:** Dent has Gordon's family at the place Rachel died. He calls Gordon: "You're about to know what my suffering is really like." Gordon arrives — Dent cracks him with gun. Dent's philosophy: "The world is cruel. The only morality in a cruel world is chance." Three coin flips: Batman (tails — shot in gut), Dent himself (heads — lives), Gordon's son (flipped — Batman tackles Dent over the edge). All three fall. Batman catches boy. Dent lands dead, neck broken. Coin stops spinning — GOOD SIDE UP.
**Key Lines:**
- DENT: "This is where they brought her, Gordon. This is where she suffered. This is where she died."
- DENT: "You thought we could be decent men in an indecent world. You thought we could lead by example. You thought the rules could be bent but not break... you were wrong. The world is cruel."
- DENT: "The only morality in a cruel world is chance. Unbiased. Unprejudiced. Fair."
- BATMAN: "Nothing fair ever came out of the barrel of a gun, Dent."
- BATMAN: "What happened to Rachel wasn't chance. We decided to act. We three. We knew the risks and we acted as one. We are all responsible for the consequences."
- DENT: "Then why was it only me who lost everything?"
- DENT: "The Joker chose me!" / BATMAN: "Because you were the best of us. He wanted to prove that even someone as good as you could fall."
- DENT: "And he was right."
**Visual Intel:** Burnt warehouse — hole in floor, charred beams, moonlight. Dent half-lit (good side normal, dark side horrific). Gun to boy's head. Coin flips. Fall from height. Dent dead at bottom — coin good side up.

### Scene 30 — THE DARK KNIGHT (pp. 137–141)
**Location:** Burnt warehouse → Dent's funeral (insert) → Gordon rooftop with axe (insert) → Alfred with letter (insert) → Fox at sonar machine (insert) → dockside rooftops → Gotham streets
**Characters:** Batman, Gordon, James Jr., Alfred, Fox
**Beat:** THE ENDING. Gordon: "The Joker won." Batman decides to take the fall for Dent's murders. "They can never know what he did." Batman turns Dent's head to show the good side. Gordon: "You can't." Batman: "Yes, I can... because I'm not a hero, like Dent. I killed those people. That's what I can be." INTERCUT: Gordon at funeral, Alfred burns Rachel's letter, Fox types his name into sonar machine (self-destructs), Gordon destroys bat-signal with axe. Batman limps to bat-pod, rides into the light. James: "Why's he running, Dad?" Gordon: "Because we have to chase him."
**Key Lines:**
- GORDON: "The Joker won."
- BATMAN: "No. But the Joker cannot win."
- BATMAN: "Gotham needs its true hero."
- BATMAN: "You either die a hero or live long enough to see yourself become the villain. I can do those things because I'm not a hero, like Dent. I killed those people. That's what I can be."
- BATMAN: "I'm whatever Gotham needs me to be."
- GORDON (V.O.): "...a hero. Not the hero we deserved — the hero we needed. Nothing less than a knight. Shining..."
- GORDON (V.O.): "...he's the hero Gotham deserves, but not the one it needs right now. So we'll hunt him, because he can take it. Because he's not our hero..."
- GORDON (V.O.): "...he's a silent guardian, a watchful protector... a dark knight."
- BATMAN (V.O.): "Because sometimes the truth isn't good enough... sometimes, people deserve more."
- JAMES: "He didn't do anything wrong!" / "Why's he running, Dad?!"
- GORDON: "Because we have to chase him."
**Visual Intel:** Batman turns Dent's head — good side up. Gordon with axe on rooftop, bat-signal sparking. Alfred burning letter by firelight. Fox typing name — machine flashes "SELF-DESTRUCT WARNINGS" then dies. Batman limping between shipping containers, bleeding. Bat-pod streaking through underground streets, cape fluttering. Blinding light. CUT TO BLACK.
